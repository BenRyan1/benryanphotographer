The Artist

A Lifetime of
Seeing Differently

With over four decades behind the lens, I've come to understand that photography is not the art of capturing reality — it's the art of revealing the reality hidden within ordinary moments. Every image begins with silence, with waiting, with the willingness to be surprised.

Based in Santa Barbara, California, my work spans the vast visual landscape of the American West — its oceanic light, its granite silence, its human texture. I bring to each image the same cross-disciplinary curiosity that has shaped my career across music, technology, and the visual arts.

My photography is available as museum-quality archival prints, as limited-edition artist proofs, and through bespoke commissions for collectors, institutions, and private clients who value the irreplaceable.

The Craft

From Vision to Wall

Every print begins with intention and ends with precision. Here is how I work.

01 — Vision
The Seeing

Photography begins before the camera appears. I spend time with a location — its rhythm, its light cycles, its human texture — until the right moment becomes inevitable.

02 — Capture
The Moment

Shot on medium-format digital or large-format film depending on the project's demands. I favor natural light, minimal intervention, and the willingness to wait as long as the image requires.

03 — Development
The Refinement

Post-processing is treated as a darkroom craft, not a correction. Color, tone, and luminosity are developed to honor what the eye actually saw — not what the sensor recorded.

04 — Edition
The Object

Printed on Hahnemühle Photo Rag or Fine Art Pearl, using archival pigment inks rated for 100+ years. Each print is signed, numbered, embossed, and issued with a certificate of authenticity.
