Provenance & Authenticity
Every Print Comes
With Its Story

Each limited edition print ships with a hand-signed, embossed Certificate of Authenticity documenting the work's full provenance — title, series, edition number, paper stock, and date of production.

The certificate is printed on archival cotton rag, signed in the artist's hand, and embossed with the studio seal. It travels with the print as the permanent provenance record for your collection.

Once an edition is closed, the file is permanently deleted. This commitment to edition integrity is unconditional — no further prints are ever produced.
